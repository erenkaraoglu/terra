BASICS

When diving into aquarium care, a few key things to keep in mind include regular water testing to maintain optimal conditions for your aquatic companions. Be cautious not to overfeed, as excess food can lead to water quality issues. Keep an eye on the temperature, ensuring it stays within the suitable range for your fish. Regular water changes are crucial to eliminate accumulated toxins and maintain a healthy environment. Finally, be mindful of compatibility when selecting tank mates to prevent conflicts. With these basics in mind, you'll be on your way to providing a thriving and balanced home for your aquarium inhabitants.
